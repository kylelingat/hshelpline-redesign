You're not alone

Waipahu is a place of culutre, diversity, and beauty. We understand what you're going through, and we understand the pain that comes with it. However, there is hope. We, the people of Waipahu High School, in light of the people who feel as if all hope is lost, created this website to show you that we care.

The people of Waipahu suffer hardships daily, but they pull through and thrive. You can to. Help is always here for you. There are people who are more than willing to listen, and it's at your fingertips right now.
